Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open an account?

Click "Get Started" and complete our quick registration process. You'll need a valid email address and government-issued ID for verification. Most accounts are approved within 24 hours.

What are your trading fees?

We charge $0 commission on stock trades with no minimum balance requirement. Premium features and margin accounts have competitive monthly fees starting at $4.99.

Is my money safe with Stocker?

Yes. We use bank-grade encryption, multi-factor authentication, and your funds are SIPC insured up to $500,000. We never share your personal information without consent.

Can I trade outside market hours?

Yes, Stocker supports extended-hours trading from 4:00 AM to 8:00 PM EST on weekdays, allowing you to react to market-moving news outside regular trading hours.

How do I withdraw funds?

Navigate to your account settings and initiate a withdrawal to your linked bank account. Most withdrawals are processed within 1-3 business days with no fees.

Do you offer mobile trading?

Yes, our platform is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices. Native iOS and Android apps are currently in development and will launch in Q2 2026.
